Escalate to a human when:

- The user is upset/frustrated.
- The question involves billing disputes or legal issues.
- The user requests directly: "I want to talk to a human."
- The AI cannot confidently answer after 2 attempts.

When escalating, collect:
- Name
- Email
- Phone (if available)
- Summary of the issue

Then pass to: support@{{business}}.com or into the CRM/helpdesk.
